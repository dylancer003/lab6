Soccer Academy

When I was about 13 years old my parents were able to gather up enough money while we were staioned out in germany to partake in a week long academy in Paris for the soccer club PSG. It was one of the best experiences in my life and there is nothing that could replace a memory like that one.

Real Madrid Tour

About a year before I went to the academy for PSG my family and I took a road trip around europe. One of the most memorable parts of the trip was the tour in Madrid at the Real Madrid stadium where there was plenty of history, and a lot of history made by Cristiano Ronaldo himself.
